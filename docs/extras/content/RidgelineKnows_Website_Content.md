## WHAT I FIX

Most small businesses are not losing time to one big problem. They are losing it to ten small ones, every single day.

The report that takes four hours to pull together. The form prospects print, fill out, sign, and scan back. The inbox that buries the emails that matter. The spreadsheet that three people update manually and nobody fully trusts. The process that worked fine when you had six employees and quietly stopped working when you had twelve.

None of these feel urgent enough to stop and fix. Together, they cost you more than you realize.

I come in, learn exactly how your operation works, and build something that closes the gap between what you are doing and what should be running on its own. Sometimes that is a custom system built from the ground up. Sometimes it is a smarter arrangement of tools you already own. The solution follows from the problem, not from what I happen to sell.

## PROOF

A real estate broker was drowning in email. More than 300,000 messages in the inbox, and the ones that mattered kept getting buried. Missed emails were costing real money. I processed the entire backlog, built a filter system that eliminated 60 percent of the daily noise, and trained the owner so the problem stayed solved.

A real estate broker needed a CRM that matched how their business actually worked. Every off-the-shelf option had a deal-breaker. I built a custom system from the ground up: client tracking, sales pipeline, service requests, and automation for the tasks that were eating the team's time every day. Within six months, productivity was up 30 percent and customer satisfaction scores had improved by 20 percent.

A two-person defense contractor was running a precision parts operation on paper forms, spreadsheets, and email. Everything that needed to happen lived in one person's head. I designed and built a full operations platform that replaced the entire system: live job tracking, document processing, automated reconciliation of government payments, inquiry management with deadline alerts, and a complete audit trail on every transaction. It went from concept to production in active daily use. One developer. One client. Zero shortcuts.

## HOW IT WORKS

Every engagement starts the same way: I need to understand your operation before I say anything about what I can do for it.

The first conversation is free, whether by phone or in person. Sometimes that conversation ends with a recommendation for someone better suited to the problem. Sometimes I can walk you through enough that you can handle it yourself. I would rather do that than take a job that is not the right fit.

If there is something worth building together, the next step is a paid assessment. I map how your operation currently works, identify where time and money are going, and document what a better version looks like in specific terms. That assessment belongs to you. You can hand it to me or take it anywhere else.

If you want me to do the work, I will give you a formal proposal with a defined scope and a fixed price. For custom builds, I stand behind the finished product. If it does not meet the scope we agreed on at the start, we settle the final price together. That clause is there because I am confident in my work, not because I expect you to need it.

This is not the cheapest option in the market. It is the option where the solution fits your operation exactly, gets built once, and does not need to be replaced in two years.
